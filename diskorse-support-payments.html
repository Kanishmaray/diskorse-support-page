Payments & Stripe

How Diskorse actually sends you money. Everything here is about Stripe: connecting it, how it handles different countries, and the fees Stripe itself charges.

Getting set up

Connecting Stripe

Stripe is Diskorse's payment provider. Your Stripe Express account is created automatically once you're eligible to receive a payout — for example, by accepting a paid Problem Solver invitation.

How do I connect Stripe to receive payments on Diskorse?+

When you accept a paid Problem Solver invitation (or become eligible to receive a payout), Diskorse automatically creates a Stripe Express account for you and redirects you to Stripe's secure onboarding process.

You'll complete Stripe's verification steps directly on Stripe's platform. Diskorse never collects or stores your banking information.

What information does Stripe require during onboarding?+

Stripe Express typically requires identity verification, including your name, date of birth, and address, along with your bank account or payout details. If you're registering as a business, Stripe may also request basic company information. The exact requirements depend on your country and Stripe's compliance regulations.

Where it works

Stripe availability by country

Stripe supports most major countries, but its exact feature set and requirements vary from one to the next.

How does Stripe work for creators in the United States?+

Creators in the United States receive the most complete Stripe experience, including fast bank payouts, support for all major cards and popular US payment methods, and options for same-day balance availability where eligible.

What if Stripe isn't supported in my country?+

If Stripe Connect isn't available in your country, you can still browse the platform and participate in discussions. However, you won't be able to receive payouts until you have access to a supported Stripe Connect account.

Diskorse settles payments internally in GBP and, where Stripe Connect is supported, Stripe FX Quotes automatically converts payouts into your local currency.

Can users outside the US pay using local payment methods?+

Yes. Stripe Checkout automatically displays the most relevant local payment methods based on the customer's country and currency, such as iDEAL, Bancontact, and others. These payment options are provided automatically by Stripe.

What Stripe charges

Stripe's fees

Stripe charges standard processing fees for handling each transaction. Diskorse covers these fees on your behalf, so they aren't charged separately to you.

What are Stripe's transaction fees?+

Diskorse covers Stripe's payment processing fees, so they are not charged separately to users. Stripe's standard published processing rates apply, which may vary depending on the payment method and region.

See Pricing & Fees for what Diskorse itself charges on top of this.

Does Stripe handle sales tax?+

Yes. Prices shown on Diskorse exclude applicable taxes, Stripe Tax calculates and adds the right tax automatically at checkout based on the buyer's location.

After the sale

How payouts work

When will I receive my payout?+

For Problem Solver rewards, once the creator publishes the idea and the escrow is released, the funds are transferred to your Stripe Connect account. From there, Stripe sends the payout to your bank according to its standard payout schedule, which is typically between 2–7 business days depending on your country and account status.

You can view your full payout history and balance at any time from your Stripe dashboard, linked from your Diskorse Payments settings.

Will I be notified when my payout is sent?+

Yes. Diskorse will send you an in-app notification once the escrow transfer has been completed. Stripe will also provide payout updates through email and your Stripe dashboard.
