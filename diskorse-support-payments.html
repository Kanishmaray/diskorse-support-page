Getting set up

Connecting Stripe

Stripe is Diskorse's payment provider. It takes a few minutes to connect, and you'll need it before you can receive money from Counsel, Vault, or paid Problem Solver collaboration.

How do I connect Stripe to receive payments on Diskorse?+

Before you start, Stripe will ask for details about you or your business and your customer support information.

Note: Your support phone number, email, and address may appear on subscriber receipts and card statements. You can update these after Stripe onboarding is complete.

  1. Go to Settings and select Payments.
  2. Select Connect Stripe. You will be taken to Stripe's setup flow.
  3. Enter the requested details, including a registered address, personal or business information, and a bank account for payouts.
  4. Once your Stripe account is set up, you will return to Diskorse where you can turn on paid access.

What information does Stripe actually need from me?+

Typically your legal name or business name, a registered address, a bank account for payouts, and identity verification documents. Exactly what's required depends on your country.

Does Stripe handle sales tax?+

Yes. Prices shown on Diskorse exclude applicable taxes, Stripe Tax calculates and adds the right tax automatically at checkout based on the buyer's location.

After the sale

How payouts work

When do I actually get the money?+

Once Stripe is connected, payouts from paid transactions go directly to your bank account. Timing follows Stripe's standard payout schedule, typically a few business days after each transaction, and can take longer for your first payout.

You can view your full payout history and balance at any time from your Stripe dashboard, linked from your Diskorse Payments settings.

Will I be notified when a payout is on its way?+

Yes. Stripe notifies you by email once a payout is sent, with the amount and estimated arrival date.
