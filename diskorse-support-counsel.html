Why this matters right now

AI can answer almost anything. It has never actually done the thing.

Instead of booking a long, expensive engagement just to ask one real question, you can go straight to someone who has skin in the game. The point of Counsel: judgment that comes from having actually done it, not a generic answer.
What is a Room, from an expert's perspective?+

Your Room is where your Counsel offering lives, along with any Explainers or debates that build your reputation before someone ever books a session. It's also where your Vouches and reviews show up, so clients can see you're worth trusting before they pay.

Setting one up costs nothing. See the full explanation on Rooms & Basics.
