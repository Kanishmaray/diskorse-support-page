Rooms & the Basics

Everything else on Diskorse, Problem Solver, Counsel, Vault, Explainers, lives inside your Room. Here's what a Room actually is and how it works.

What is a Room?+

A Room is your space on Diskorse. It holds everything you create: debates, Explainers, Vault articles, Problem Solvers, and Counsel sessions, all in one place other people can find you by.

Who can post in my Room?+

You're always in control of what you publish. Other people can show up inside your Room too, applying to a Problem Solver you've posted, booking a Counsel session, or leaving a review, but they can't publish content into it directly.

What can I do in my Room?+

What you can actually do inside it, publishing Explainers, selling on Vault, running a Problem Solver, offering Counsel, depends on your plan. See Pricing & Fees for what unlocks what.

How do I monetize my Room?+

A few ways, depending on what you're here to do:

  • Sell individual articles or Bundles on Vault
  • Offer paid sessions through Counsel
  • Run a paid Problem Solver and get paid to contribute to someone else's
  • Respond to Brand Queries as a verified brand

Each of these has its own plan requirement, see Pricing & Fees for the breakdown.

Credibility

Vouches

Vouches are Diskorse's credibility layer, a signal to other users, clients, and brands that you're worth trusting in a specific category.

What is a Vouch?+

A Vouch is a mark of credibility someone gives you after you've actually helped them, in a specific category. Vouches show up on your profile and signal to potential Counsel clients, Vault readers, and brands that you're worth trusting, so they carry real weight beyond just a number.

How do I earn a Vouch?+

You can't just be handed a Vouch out of nowhere, someone has to have actually interacted with something you did in that category first. That could mean:

  • Contributing to someone's Problem Solver and having them vouch for your input afterward
  • Winning a Debate Arena in a given category
  • Wrapping up a Counsel session the client was happy with

The Vouch is then tied to that specific category, so someone can build a strong reputation in, say, skincare, without it automatically implying credibility in, say, finance.

Is there a limit to how often someone can vouch for me?+

Yes, there are limits so Vouches stay meaningful instead of turning into a popularity contest. To confirm: cap is twice per category per person per month. The same person can vouch for you in the same category, but only up to that limit, they can't just keep stacking Vouches on top of each other.

How are my Vouches displayed on my profile?+

By default, the categories where you have the most Vouches show up at the top of your profile. You can also customize this yourself, choosing which categories to highlight and reordering them, rather than always being stuck with whatever ranks highest automatically.

Free features

A few things you can do on Diskorse without a paid plan, useful on their own and often the first step toward building the credibility that paid features reward.

Free

Debate Arena

A structured debate that lives inside your Room. You invite participants, arguments go up during a set window, then the community votes and a winner is announced. Winning builds your Vouches and credibility, which feeds directly into Counsel bookings, Vault trust, and Brand Query responses.

Free

Polls

A lightweight way to get a read on an idea without setting up a paid Problem Solver. Anyone can vote or drop a quick opinion, public, fast, and free, useful when you want broad reaction rather than a deep paid conversation.

Free to read

Explainers

Ongoing posts inside a creator's Room, similar to a blog, and how most creators build an audience over time. Reading them is free for everyone. Publishing your own needs the Creator plan.
