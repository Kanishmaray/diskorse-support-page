Reading vs. publishing

Free to read, not free to publish

Anyone can read Explainers for free. Publishing your own needs the Creator plan ($12/mo), the same plan that unlocks Bundles on Vault.

Worth double checking: this means the audience building step (Explainers) sits on a higher tier than selling an individual Vault article, which only needs the cheaper Engage plan. If the goal is to get creators publishing early, it might be worth reconsidering which plan unlocks Explainers.
