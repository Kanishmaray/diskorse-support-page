Our commission

On top of your plan, Diskorse takes a cut once a paid transaction actually happens.

What does Diskorse take from each sale?+

10% on Vault sales and Counsel sessions, for anyone on the Creator, Brand, or Brand+ plan. Stripe takes its own separate processing fee on top of that, covered on the Payments & Stripe page.

Flagged to confirm: the commission list only names Creator, Brand, and Brand+, but the Engage plan also allows selling individual Vault articles. Worth double checking whether Engage sales carry the same 10% or are commission free.
Can I set my own prices and discounts?+

Yes. Whether it's a Counsel session, a Vault article, a Bundle, or what you pay a Problem Solver collaborator, you set the price and you decide if and when to offer a discount. Diskorse's commission is a percentage of whatever price you set, so it moves with you.

How does annual billing work?+

Paying annually saves you 2 months compared to paying monthly all year, worked out to 10 months' cost for 12 months of access.
