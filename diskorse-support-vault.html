How Vault & Bundles works

Vault is where you sell your writing directly, a single article, or a full Bundle packaged like an issue or a small book. No flat subscription splitting your earnings across the whole platform. Readers pay for exactly what they want to read, and you keep a direct share of every sale.

Why Vault beats a flat subscription

A fixed monthly subscription charges readers for a mix of work they may only partly want, and spreads a creator's earnings thin across everyone on the platform. Vault works differently.

Flat subscription

Readers pay one fee for everything, whether they read three articles or thirty. Creators split a shared pool based on engagement, not direct sales.

Vault & Bundles

Readers pay for exactly what they want to read. Creators keep a direct share of every sale tied to their own work.

Pricing & how it works

Which plan do I need to sell on Vault?+

Selling an individual article needs the Engage plan ($5/mo). Bundles need the Creator plan ($12/mo). See Pricing & Fees for the full plan breakdown.

Do I need a Room to sell on Vault?+

Yes. Everything you publish, free or paid, lives inside your Room. See Rooms & Basics if you haven't set one up yet.

What is a custom bundle?+

Think of it as building your own magazine. Instead of subscribing to one creator, you pick individual articles from across Diskorse and pay for exactly what you want to read.

Do I need a paid plan to buy an article?+

No. Buying and reading is free for everyone, no plan required. Paid plans are only about what you're allowed to create and sell.
